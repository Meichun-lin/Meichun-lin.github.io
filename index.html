Meichun Lin

Welcome to my site! I am an Assistant Professor of Operations Management at Lee Kong Chian School of Business, Singapore Management University.

I obtained my Ph.D. at Sauder School of Business, University of British Columbia, where I was advised by Tim Huh and Michael Jong Kim.

My research focuses on data-driven decision making with applications such as dynamic pricing and inventory management.

Prior to my Ph.D., I received my bachelor degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.
